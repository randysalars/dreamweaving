## Getting Started

### Recommended First Steps

1. **Read the Companion Guide** (at least the first 4 chapters)
2. **Create Your Practice Space** - Quiet, comfortable, distraction-free
3. **Start with Session 1** - Do it 2-3 times before moving on
4. **Progress Sequentially** - The sessions build on each other

### Equipment Recommended

- **Headphones** - Any comfortable headphones work well
- **Eye mask** - Optional but helpful for blocking light
- **Comfortable position** - Lying down or seated with back support

### Best Practices

- Practice at the same time daily (builds habit)
- Morning practice sets tone for the day
- Don't practice when overtired (you'll fall asleep)
- Journal briefly after each session
- Give yourself transition time after practice
