# Garden of Eden - Session Notes

**Path-Working Type:** Archetypal Journey
**Duration:** 25-30 minutes
**Theme:** Innocence, Wisdom, and Wholeness
**Created:** 2025

---

## Overview

A transformational journey through the Garden of Eden exploring the integration of:
- Original innocence and purity
- Conscious discernment and wisdom
- Divine wholeness and unity

This path-working reframes the Garden of Eden myth as an internal journey of consciousness, where the "fall" becomes an opportunity for conscious awakening and integration.

---

## Script Structure

### 1. Pre-Talk Introduction (2-3 min)
- Welcome and safety protocols
- Setting intentions
- Preparation instructions

### 2. Induction (3-5 min)
- Progressive relaxation (head to toe)
- Breathing exercises (4-4-6 pattern)
- Countdown deepening (10 to 1)

### 3. Main Journey (15-20 min)

#### The Meadow of Innocence
- Awakening to original purity
- Release of shame and self-consciousness
- White deer messenger
- Gift: White feather (representing innocence)

#### The Path of Temptation: Serpent Wisdom
- Reframing temptation as discernment
- Meeting the serpent as wisdom teacher
- Tree of Knowledge as conscious choice
- Gift: Golden apple (representing discernment)

#### The Tree of Life: Integration & Wholeness
- Chakra activation and balancing
- Drinking from the Water of Life
- Full rainbow spectrum integration
- Gift: Rainbow seed (representing wholeness)

#### The Divine Presence
- Meeting the Divine within
- Recognition of non-separation
- Integration of all three gifts

### 4. Integration & Return (2-3 min)
- Gentle awakening process
- Counting up (1 to 10)
- Grounding in present awareness

### 5. Post-Hypnotic Anchors (2-3 min)
Five powerful daily-life anchors installed

---

## The Five Post-Hypnotic Anchors

1. **Three Sacred Breaths** - Return to innocence anytime
2. **Pause for Discernment** - Access wisdom before choices
3. **Rainbow Seed Meditation** - Daily wholeness practice
4. **Garden Doorway** - Instant return to inner peace
5. **Divine Embrace** - Self-comfort and reassurance

- **Innocence vs. Shame**: Releasing learned shame and returning to original purity
- **Temptation as Teacher**: Reframing temptation as invitation to conscious choice
