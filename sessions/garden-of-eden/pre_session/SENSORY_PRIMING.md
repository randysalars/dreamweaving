## üåø Aromatherapy Protocol

### Recommended Essential Oils
Use **ONE** of the following, not multiple:

1. **Lavender** (Lavandula angustifolia)
   - Effect: Calming, parasympathetic activation
   - Best for: Anxiety reduction, sleep preparation
   - Application: 2-3 drops in diffuser, 30 min before session

2. **Sandalwood** (Santalum album)
   - Effect: Grounding, meditative, sacred space
   - Best for: Spiritual connection, root chakra activation
   - Application: 2-3 drops in diffuser or apply diluted to wrists

3. **Frankincense** (Boswellia carterii)
   - Effect: Deepens breath, crown chakra activation
   - Best for: Mystical experiences, divine connection
   - Application: 1-2 drops in diffuser (potent)

4. **Jasmine** (Jasminum officinale)
   - Effect: Heart-opening, sensual, euphoric
   - Best for: Emotional release, innocence work
   - Application: 1-2 drops diffused or as perfume

### Application Guidelines
- Start diffusing **30 minutes before** session
- Keep scent **subtle** (not overpowering)
- If using topical: dilute in carrier oil (coconut, jojoba)
- Avoid if pregnant or sensitive to fragrances
- Option: Natural incense (sandalwood, nag champa)

## üí° Light & Color Protocol

### Pre-Session Lighting (15-30 min before)
- **Color**: Violet or deep indigo
- **Brightness**: Dim, indirect (like candles)
- **Purpose**: Activate pineal gland, prepare for visionary states
- **Options**:
  - LED color-changing bulbs
  - Himalayan salt lamp (orange/pink)
  - Violet LED strip lights
  - Candles (flame flicker aids hypnagogia)

### During Session Lighting
Choose ONE approach:

1. **Complete Darkness** (Recommended)
   - Use eye mask or blackout curtains
   - Enhances internal visuals
   - Maximizes theta wave production

2. **Ganzfeld Effect** (Advanced)
   - Soft, diffuse white or red light
   - No patterns, no shadows
   - Creates sensory deprivation
   - DIY: cut ping-pong balls in half, place over eyes with soft lamp

3. **Pulsing Light** (Optional, Advanced)
   - Programmable LEDs at 6-8 Hz (theta frequency)
   - Through closed eyelids
   - Entrains visual cortex
   - **Warning**: Not for epilepsy

### Post-Session Lighting
- Gradual brightening
- Start with warm orange/yellow
- Slow transition to normal light over 5-10 min
- Avoid harsh fluorescent immediately
