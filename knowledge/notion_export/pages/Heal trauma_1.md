- 1. Safe Haven Pathworking: Visualize yourself in a safe and comforting environment, allowing yourself to process and heal wounds in this protective setting.
- 2. Light of Release Pathworking: Picture a bright, healing light flooding your body, washing away the pain and scars from past traumas, and recharging your body's energy centers.
- 3. Time Rewind Pathworking: Imagine stepping back in time before your traumas occurred. Reinforce this positive state. Over time, let this positively influence your present reality.
- 4. Empathetic Echo Pathworking: Create a space where you can echo your feelings without judgment. The act of expressing can go a long way in healing.
- 5. Forgiveness Flow Pathworking: Try to cultivate feelings of forgiveness towards the person or situation that caused your scars. This is not about excusing them but allowing yourself to let go and move forward.
- 6. [[Tree of Transformation Pathworking]]: Visualize a tree where each leaf represents a negative experience. As leaves fall, see them decompose, fertilizing new, positive growth.
- 7. Soothing Stream Pathworking: Picture yourself bathing in a stream of soothing, healing water, washing away past hurts and replacing them with fresh energy and positivity.
- 8. Cosmic Caregiver Pathworking: Imagine a cosmic caregiver addressing your wounds with universal love and wisdom, encouraging a sense of peace and healing.
- 9. Ocean of Oblivion Pathworking: Visualize throwing stones (symbolizing traumas and negative experiences) into an endless ocean, acknowledging them then letting them sink into oblivion.
- 10. Golden Glow Pathworking: Picture your being enveloped by a golden glow symbolizing divine love and healing power, transforming your traumas into wisdom and strength.
- These exercises can help you process past hardships and create a path of healing. Please remember to seek professional help if trauma is severely impacting your daily life; these exercises should be used in conjunction with, not instead of, professional treatment.
