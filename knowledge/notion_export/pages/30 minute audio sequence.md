## 🔧 4. Layering and Mixing Tips

- Binaural beats:
  - Theta left ear = 200 Hz, right ear = 206 Hz → 6 Hz perceived beat
  - Alpha left ear = 220 Hz, right ear = 228 Hz → 8 Hz perceived beat
- Isochronic gamma bursts:
  - Short 30–60 Hz pulses, 2–3 sec duration, <10% total amplitude
  - Slight detune per channel (left 35 Hz, right 37 Hz) to enhance spatial effect
- Environmental layer:
  - Stereo ambient field, soft panning, volume at ~20% of main signal
- Phase alignment:
  - Theta + alpha in phase → smooth low-frequency base
  - Gamma independent, non-phase locked → bursts feel spontaneous
