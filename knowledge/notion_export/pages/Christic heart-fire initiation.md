## 1. Journey — “Walking Into the Heart of the Logos”

### 🕊️ Stage I — The Desert of Silence (Purification)

You step into an ancient Judean desert at twilight.

Red-gold sand glows beneath your feet. A warm wind carries the scent of frankincense.

A distant bell tolls — each vibration like a heartbeat of eternity.

You walk toward a small stone hermitage carved into a cliff.

Inside, a single candle burns. The flame bows inward and then rises, as if greeting you.

A whisper fills the room:

“Prepare your heart, beloved.”

Breath deepens.

The room expands into a limitless golden void.

---

### 🕊️ Stage II — The Encounter with the Living Flame (Ignition)

A pillar of white-gold Christ-Light appears before you — not a figure but a radiant Presence.

The Light approaches your chest.

It touches your sternum.

A sudden warmth—like liquid sunrise—spreads outward.

Your ribs become translucent.

Inside your chest, a small ember ignites: the Christic Fire Seed.

It pulses.

Each pulse sends ripples of molten love through your body.

You hear:

“This flame is Me within you.”

---

### 🕊️ Stage III — The Heart Becoming a Sun (Expansion)

The ember grows.

It becomes a orb of blazing white-gold fire.

Your chest opens into a sun, radiating:

- compassion
- courage
- truth
- mercy
- justice
- resurrection force
Your entire torso becomes a cathedral of living flame.

Stained-glass visions unfold around you:

Christ walking on water…

Christ transfigured on Tabor…

Christ breaking bread…

Christ rolling away the stone…

Each image dissolves into pure light and merges with your heart.

---

### 🕊️ Stage IV — Christos Within (Communion)

The Presence steps into you.

Feet become His feet.

Hands become His hands.

Heart becomes His heart.

You do not lose identity —

your identity becomes infused, transfigured, made luminescent.

A voice rises from within your bloodstream:

“You are the living temple of My fire.”

The flame stabilizes into a permanent Christic inner-sun, neither too hot nor too faint.

---

### 🕊️ Stage V — Anointing and Sending (Empowerment)

A Seraph approaches with a coal from the heavenly altar.

It places the coal on your tongue.

Instead of pain, it feels like

warm honeyed lightning,

infusing your speech with truth and blessing.

Your hands ignite with soft flame.

Your eyes shine with steady clarity.

Your spine hums with holy electricity.

You are sent out through a doorway shaped like a cross made of pure radiance.

A final blessing:

“Carry My fire into all worlds.”
