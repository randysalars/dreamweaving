# 🌟 PART I — The Core Formula (The “3-Point CTR Engine”)

Every Dreamweaver thumbnail defaults to this repeatable viral structure:

## 1. The Focal Mythic Subject (Center or Right-Side Focus)

Placement rule:

- 70% of viral thumbnails place the primary subject on the right third.
- The eye is drawn right → thumbnail is perceived as more dynamic.
Subject options:

- Archetype (Alchemist, Navigator, Soul Star)
- Portal
- Viewer silhouette
- Celestial being
Needs:

- Clear silhouette
- High contrast
- Dynamic emotional posture
Emotions to express:

- Awe
- Hope
- Mystery
- Revelation
- Courage
- Wonder
---

## 2. The Viral “Impossible Moment”

This is the DREAMWEAVER SECRET WEAPON.

Each thumbnail should capture a single moment that feels:

- Impossible
- Magical
- Transformational
- Intriguing
- Symbolically charged
Examples:

- A glowing portal splitting the sky
- A massive celestial being emerging from fog
- The Navigator’s lantern turning an unusual color
- A runic sigil floating in front of the viewer
- A mirror reflecting a different self
- Reality bending around the character
This creates the “I need to see what’s going on here” impulse.

---

## 3. The Signature Dreamweaver Symbol (Small, Branded, Consistent)

Add ONE small iconic symbol that:

- Handles branding
- Builds universe continuity
- Helps fans identify series
- Encourages “symbol hunting”
Good placements:

- Top-left
- Bottom-left
Symbols:

- The Dreamweaver Sigil
- Lantern icon
- Spiral of Becoming
- Fractal Crown
Included at ~7% of thumbnail area, semi-transparent or glowing.

# 🌬️ PART IV — Viral Micro-Elements (CTR Boosters)

These micro-elements elevate 15–40% CTR increases:

## 1. Eye Direction

Eyes looking into the portal increase clicks because viewers follow gaze.

## 2. Atmospheric Fog + Light Shafts

Creates cinematic depth + mystery.

## 3. Edge Framing Glow

Soft glow around silhouette sharply increases contrast on mobile.

## 4. Subtle Motion Blur

Communicates action, which triggers click curiosity.

## 5. Sigil Glow Pulses

Symbols feel alive → more worldbuilding immersion.

## 6. Teal/Gold Rim Light

A professional, heroic look.
