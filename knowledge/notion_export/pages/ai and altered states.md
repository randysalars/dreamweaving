Sound is one of the most powerful ASC triggers.

AI can generate:

- Binaural beats tuned to specific frequencies

- Isochronic pulses for cleaner entrainment
- Psychoacoustic fields (spatialized 3D audio)
- Shamanic-style rhythmic patterns generated algorithmically
- Brainwave-following compositions that shift gradually to lead a user deeper
For spiritual ASC, the most effective ranges are:

- Theta (4–8 Hz) → hypnagogic, visionary
- Delta (0.5–4 Hz) → deep trance, surrender
- Gamma (30–80 Hz) → high-integration mystical unity
AI can blend all of these into evolving sonic rituals.
