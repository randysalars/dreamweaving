- There are several other pantheons throughout human history and across cultures. Here are some examples:
- 1. Norse Pantheon - Includes gods like Odin, Thor, and Freya from Scandinavia.
- 2. Egyptian Pantheon - Includes deities like Ra, Isis, Anubis, and others from Ancient Egypt.
- 3. Sumerian Pantheon - Includes deities like Inanna, Enlil, and Enki from Ancient Mesopotamia.
- 4. Hindu Pantheon - A broad range of gods like Shiva, Vishnu, Kali, and Ganesha from India.
- 5. Aztec Pantheon - Includes gods like Quetzalcoatl, Tezcatlipoca, and others from Ancient Mexico.
- 6. Mayan Pantheon - Encompasses gods like Itzamna, Ix Chel, and Kukulcan from Ancient Mesoamerica.
- 7. Chinese Pantheon - Includes deities like Yu Huang (Jade Emperor), Guan Yin, and others from China.
  - Japanese Pantheon (Shinto and Buddhist) - Includes deities like Amaterasu, Susanoo, and a range of Bodhisattvas and Buddhas.
- 9. Oceanic Pantheons - Includes a range of cultures with their own deities, like Pele in Hawaiian mythology, and others from cultures such as the Maori of New Zealand.
- 10. African Pantheons - Includes a range of cultures with their own gods like the Yoruba Orishas, the gods of Ancient Nubia, and many others.
