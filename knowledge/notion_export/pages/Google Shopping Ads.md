# ⭐ Phase 1 — Set Up Google Merchant Center (GMC)

### 1. Create your Merchant Center account

Go to:

👉 https://merchants.google.com/

Use the same Google account tied to your website.

### 2. Business Information Setup

Enter:

- Country (USA)
- Business name (“Dreamweaver” or “Salars Dreamweaving Studio”)
- Website URL
### 3. Verify & Claim Your Website

You’ll verify the domain using one of these:

- HTML tag on your homepage
- DNS TXT record
- Google Analytics
- Google Tag Manager
If your website is Shopify / WooCommerce / custom Next.js, I’ll tailor this step.

### 4. Enable “Shopping Ads” + “Free Listings”

These allow products to appear:

- In Google Shopping tab
- In search results as product cards
- In YouTube Shopping (bonus)

# ⭐ Phase 4 — How to Format Dreamweaver Products for Google Shopping

This is where we make magic.

### Every Dreamweaver product needs:

1. Title
  - Format: Keyword + Dreamweaver + Type
  - Example:
    “Starlight Journey of Abundance — Dreamweaver Guided Meditation (Digital Download)”

1. Description
  - Must describe what the buyer receives.
  - Avoid claims of guaranteed outcomes (Google is strict here).
  - Focus on:
    - Experience
    - Relaxation
    - Spirituality
    - High-quality audio & artwork
1. Image
  - 800×800 minimum
  - Use your mystical portal aesthetic
  - No text overlay needed (Google rejects heavy text)
1. Price & Availability
  - Example: $7.99, In stock
1. Product Category
  Use Google’s taxonomy:

  → “Digital Goods > Digital Downloads > Audio Downloads”

  → “Digital Goods > Digital Downloads > PDF / Printables”

1. GTIN / MPN
  Digital products qualify for:

  👉 “identifier_exists = FALSE”

This bypasses barcode requirements.

# ⭐ Phase 5 — Launch Your Shopping Campaign

### In Google Ads:

1. Create new campaign → “Sales”
1. Choose Shopping → Standard Shopping (Smart Shopping requires history)
1. Link the Merchant Center
1. Set daily budget ($5–$20 to start)
1. Add product groups:
  - Guided Meditations
  - Dreamweaver Ritual Kits
  - Printables
  - Oracle Cards
### Targeting strategy:

- Start with broad targeting
- After 7–14 days, optimize based on:
  - Clicks
  - Conversions
  - Search terms
### Add retargeting (important!)

- YouTube
- Display ads
- Search retargeting
This boosts your ROAS significantly.
