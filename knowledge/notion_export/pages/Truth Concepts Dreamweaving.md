- 7. Mountain of Wisdom: Embark on a challenging mountaineering expedition. Each landmark along the way represents a valuable learning or insight, promoting continuous learning and personal growth.
- 8. The Fabric of Time: Journey across a woven tapestry, each thread a different timeline of events. Experience moments of intertwining threads symbolizing interconnectedness and legacies, fostering a conscious appreciation for past, present, and future.
