# 🜂 1. Preparation — Clearing and Centering

Before entering Layer 2:

1. Layer 1 Integration: Resolve a symbol, shadow, or insight from Deep Self.
1. Sacred Breath Alignment: Use 6–2–8 pattern (inhale 6, hold 2, exhale 8).
1. Intent Declaration: Speak aloud:
  “I call my Higher Self to meet me in clarity and love.”

1. Environment: Dimmed light, sigil present, quiet space, optional VR temple.
💡 Rationale: Preparation ensures the subtle-body channels are clear and the practitioner’s ego does not dominate the encounter.
