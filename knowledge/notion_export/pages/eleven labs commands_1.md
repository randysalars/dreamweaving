```plain text
**Putting it all together**, here’s a tiny sample that uses many of the above:


```

[whispers] Take a slow, deep breath…

…and let it go [long pause]

Feel each word… wash over you…

SLOWLY now… your shoulders drop…

Imagine a warm glow… spreading from your chest…

[sighs] Deeper… and deeper…

```plain text

**Tips for success**
1.  Start with short segments (3–5 lines), preview, then build out.
2.  Match your chosen voice’s “personality”—a neutral, warm voice will respond best to `[whispers]` and `[softly]`, whereas an energetic voice might not.
3.  Use ellipses and line‐breaks liberally to craft that hypnotic, lullaby-like rhythm.
4.  Experiment with the Stability slider: **Natural** for balance; **Creative** for maximum expressiveness.

With these tools, you can transform any hypnotic script into a polished, AI-voiced experience that feels hand-recorded—complete with pauses, inflections, warmth and pace custom-tuned to your listener’s needs.
```
